W - Ability: Wolf's Frenzy

PASSIVE: As Kindred move and attack, they build up to 100 stacks of Hunter's Vigor. At full stacks, Lamb's next basic attack restores up to 32 - 100 (based on level) health, based on Kindred's missing health. ACTIVE: Wolf Dash dashes to the target location and claims the surrounding area as his territory for the next 8.5 seconds, separating from Lamb. Wolf autonomously attacks enemies within the area, prioritizing Lamb's attack target. Wolf's basic attacks deal magic damage and scale with 50% of Kindred's bonus attack speed. Against monsters, Wolf deals 50% increased damage and his attacks both Slow icon slow his target by 50% for 2 seconds. MAGIC DAMAGE: 25 / 30 / 35 / 40 / 45 (+ 20% bonus AD) (+ 1.5% (+ 1% per Mark of the Kindred Mark) of target's current health) Wolf's Frenzy ends immediately if Lamb leaves the area.
E - Ability: Mounting Dread

ACTIVE: Lamb cripples the target unit, Slow icon slowing them by 50% for 1 second. If Lamb manages to attack the target twice with no longer than 4 seconds between each attack after casting Mounting Dread, her third attack within the following 4 seconds directs Wolf to pounce the target, dealing bonus physical damage, capped against monster at 300 bonus damage. BONUS PHYSICAL DAMAGE: 80 / 100 / 120 / 140 / 160 (+ 80% bonus AD) (+ 8% (+ 0.5% per Mark of the Kindred Mark) of target's missing health) Wolf's pounce will Critical strike icon critically strike for 50% bonus damage against targets below 15% (+ 50% critical strike chance) of their maximum health (this applies to monster as well).
